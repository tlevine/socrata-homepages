Contract Register, Updated Monthly in this Dataset Which contracts are registered in the Government Contracts Register? In accordance with the Government Procurement Act 2001, from 1 July 2012 (R17) ACT Government contracts with a value of $25,000 or above are publicly viewable on the Government Contracts Register. How do I view contracts within the Government Contracts Register? Using the 'Previous ACT Government Contracts Register (July 2003 - July 2009)' link, contracts which were loaded onto the previous contract register may be viewed and searched. Using the 'New ACT Government Contracts Register' link, contracts loaded from the 1 August 2009 can be viewed and searched. Who do I contact regarding specific contracts within the Government Contracts Register? Details of specific contracts can be obtained through the relevant department/agency. For system related enquiries, please contact SharedServicesProcurement@act.gov.au.
